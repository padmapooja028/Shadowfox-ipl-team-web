History & Highlights

Chennai Super Kings (CSK) debuted in the inaugural IPL season in 2008. Led by iconic captain MS Dhoni for many seasons, CSK has won multiple IPL titles and is known for its strong leadership, loyal fanbase (the Yellow Brigade), and strategic gameplay.

  • Multiple IPL Champions (2008, 2010, 2011, 2018, 2021)
  • Consistent playoff appearances and strong batting and spin bowling units.
